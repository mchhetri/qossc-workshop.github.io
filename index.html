QoS-SC 2016

International Workshop on Quality of Service in Smart Cities

In Conjunction with the IEEE International Conference on Collaboration and Internet Computing (CIC 2016)

Overview

The vision of Smart Cities is to use advances in information and communication technologies to enhance the quality of life by improving urban services such as transportation, energy, water, health, safety, welfare and environment, in a sustainable manner while reducing their cost of delivery. Quality can include performance, pricing, availability, reliability, security, privacy and user experience. Most Smart City services are user-centric and therefore it is essential to ensure both the Quality of Service (QoS) offered by the Smart City systems, applications and services, and the Quality of Experience (QoE) perceived by the end user.
