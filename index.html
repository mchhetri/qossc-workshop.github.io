QoS-SC 2016

International Workshop on Quality of Service in Smart Cities

In Conjunction with the IEEE International Conference on Collaboration and Internet Computing (CIC 2016)

Overview

The Smart City vision aims at exploiting advances in information and communication technologies to improve the quality of urban services such as transportation, energy, water, health, safety, welfare and environment, and through it, enhance the quality of life for its citizens. This workshop welcomes submissions on work related to Quality of Service (QoS), Quality of Protection (QoP) and Quality of Experience (QoE) in the context of Smart City systems, applications and services which include, but are not limited to the following application areas:

  • Intelligent Traffic Management
  • Smart & Efficient Energy Management
  • Smart Transportation
  • Smart Buildings
  • Smart Agriculture
  • E-Government Services
  • Emergency Response & Event Planning
  • Social Computing & Networks
  • Environment and Urban Monitoring
  • E-Health Systems
